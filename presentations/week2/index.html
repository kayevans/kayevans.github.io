Golden Ratio Web Design

How to do the Assignment

  • For this exercise, you will be creating an aesthetically pleasing webpage by applying the golden ratio to the spacing and positioning of the elements of your page.
  • The required elements include a header, a navigation bar, a sidebar, a title or logo, and a body. The body and sidebar must include copy (text), Lorem Ipsum is allowed.
  • Your font size and line height must also follow the golden ratio. To do so, enter your content width in px into this calculator and the best font size and line height will be provided for you.
  • The webpage is to be created using a 1280 x 800 px artboard in Adobe Illustrator
  • Choose a photo for your header from unsplash , the photo itself must also use the golden ratio
  • For inspiration on ways to apply the golden ratio to your webpage design, visit Canva.

How to Upload the Assignment

  1. Save your Illustrator file as a png and name “LastnameFirstname_GoldenRatio”.
  2. Go to Kay’s Repo.
  3. Click on the “upload.html” under “week 2”.
  4. Go to “Raw”, copy and paste the code into your “upload.html” under “week 2” folder.
  5. Put your png file in the unfinished “img” tag.
  6. Put your png file in your “week 2” folder.
  7. Put your first in the “h1”.
  8. Save the file, commit to master, and push origin.
